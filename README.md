
# Smart-Job-Portal
A job portal is a website, which helps in the recruitment process by bringing together both the employer and the job-seeking candidate.This system can be used as an Online Job Portal for the Placements providing to the un employees who are seeking for a job placement. Job Seeker logging into the system and he can should be able to upload their information in the form of a CV. Visitors/Company representatives logging in may also access/search any information put up by Job Seeker
